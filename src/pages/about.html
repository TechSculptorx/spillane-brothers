OUR CORE VALUES

Each and every one of our projects is treated with the same core values that characterise our company – honesty, integrity, professional advice and quality craftsmanship. In fact, honesty and trust are the very cornerstones of our business.

Safety is a process

Safety isn't a destination. There isn't a perfect combination of paperwork and planning that will guarantee safety. It is an ongoing & varying process that requires engagement, attention & understanding. It’s very easy to get overwhelmed by large documents with incomprehensible language. This leads to dis-engagement & removes the opportunity for understanding. At Spillane Brothers, we try to keep the process as simple and engaging as possible. A simple message that is understood, is many times more effective than an all bases covered essay that isn't.

Customer is king

Too often in our industry, the concept of good customer service takes a back seat to commercial concerns i.e. the pursuit of extras. This can be a cultural issue, or it can be generated by an adversarial attitude resulting from a lack of trust between the Client & Contractor (on one side, or both). Unfortunately, such attitudes have been forged, and remain, as a result of poor customer service. We believe in partnership and cooperation and will always prioritise customer service.

Solutions, solutions, solutions

We view solutions as a vital part of our service. Being solutions orientated creates momentum and energy in the right direction. It is the construction equivalent of a positive mental attitude.

Quality is a given

Quality is a given. There is nothing more to say.

Trust matters

Trust matters. Without it, the relationship between the parties is a purely contractual one. While this is of course functional, it could be so much smoother, and even pleasant, if trust and cooperation are developed and maintained.

Attitude is everything

Attitudes become personality traits in companies just the same as in individuals. Need we say more. Attitude is everything.
